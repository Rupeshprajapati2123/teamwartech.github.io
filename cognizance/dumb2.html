RULE BOOK

 Event related RULES
1) In group activities, team can accommodate maximum of 5 members.
2) No student is allowed to participate in more than 5 events.(Including individual and group activities)
3) Age limit for participants on 1 September of the academic year will be 23 years.
4) There will 3 positions in each event in WARTECH.
5) Honestly participate in each event, any illegal activity can throw you out of the competition.
 General RULES
1) Only one entry one person is allowed.
2) Volunteers are mandatory to carry their college ID.
3) Maintain cleanliness inside and outside the campus.
4) Put your trash in the dustbin instead of throwing it anywhere.
5) Do not harm greenery of campus.
